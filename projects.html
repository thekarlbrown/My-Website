I am currently developing a tool, codenamed Divination, to perform market analysis using components that include stock market algorithms, news article analysis, web scrapers, a neural network, and genetic algorithms. Portions of the code are present on Github, but I cannot share all of it publicly for intellectual property reasons. Please email me with questions/for a demos when completed.

Senior Capstone - Change the World

As part of my Senior project, I developed an Android app that utilizes a MySQL database on this server alongside a PHP Backend and Web API to enable the sharing, group evaluation, improvement and implementation of new ideas and innovation. This project won Best Senior Capstone (Thesis) out of 36 Computer Science graduates at Christopher Newport University.

CNU CS:GO Team Manager

I was Captain of multiple eSports teams at Christopher Newport University, including the Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team. I developed practice methodologies and led my team in Leagues against other Colleges, beating teams at colleges such as Princeton and CalTech. If you are at a beginner to intermediate CS:GO skill level and want to improve, my analysis and team exersizes on the announcement page are a good place to start!

Automated Mass SMTP to SMS Messaging

This tool was an experiment in sending SMS messages via SMTP en-masse to cellular devices, without having to input SMTP server details or messages at runtime or know the carrier beforehand. It is a good mass SMTP request stresser for your SMTP server, and can be quite a chuckle if consent of use is obtained. Do not violate any local/state/federal laws while using this script.
